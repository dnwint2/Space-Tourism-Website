02Meet your crew

Pilot

Victor Glover

Pilot on the first flight of the SpaceX Crew Dragon to the International Space Station. Glover is a commander in the U.S. Navy where he pilots an F/A-18. He was a crew member of Expedition 64, and served as station systems flight engineer.
