Launch Vehicle

A launch vehicle or rocket carrier is a rocket-propelled vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our Web-X carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation. Standing 150 metres tall, it's quite an awe-inspring sight on the launch pad.
